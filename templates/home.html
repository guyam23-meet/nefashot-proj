NEFASHOT

About Us

Nefashot (Souls in Hebrew) is a social initiative whose purpose is to raise awareness about the field of mental health in the public space through art, culture and dialogue-based events.

Nefashot Mental Health Festival started as a Jerusalem initiative and in the first two years the events mainly took place in Jerusalem’s public space. However, in 2020, due to the social distancing restrictions surrounding the corona pandemic, the activity expanded geographically as the events were mostly virtual. Such an unexpected reach led to nationwide involvement and, today, the Nefashot Mental Health Festival will take place in the north, the south, the center and of course in Jerusalem.
